# Aurora OSI vNext — Commercial Pricing Framework

---

## 1. PRICING PHILOSOPHY

**Core Principle:** Pricing is **outcome-independent**. Scan cost reflects survey scope (area, resolution, features), NOT geological results.

- A high-ACIF, world-class discovery pays the same as a dry scan
- A failed acquisition costs the same as successful one
- Commercial guarantee: No surprise fees based on results

---

## 2. PRICING TIERS

### Tier 1: EXPLORE
**Target:** Proof-of-concept, regional reconnaissance, low commitment

**Parameters:**
- Area: 10–500 km²
- Resolution: 250m (coarse satellite-scale)
- Live streaming: Not included
- Digital twin: Not included
- Analog validation: Optional (+$300)
- Report depth: Summary (cover page + executive summary + spatial clusters)
- Data room access: 30 days

**Base Price:** $2,500  
**Area multiplier:** 1.0 + (area_km² / 1000) × 0.0005  
**Resolution multiplier:** 1.0 (baseline)  

**Price Calculation Example (100 km² AOI):**
- Base: $2,500
- Area adjustment: 100/1000 × 0.0005 = 0.00005; very small impact
- Final: ~**$2,500** (area has minimal impact at coarse resolution)

**Feature additions:**
- Analog validation: +$300
- Data room extension (30 more days): +$100

**Total Explore tier: $2,500–$2,900**

---

### Tier 2: STANDARD
**Target:** Active exploration, operator-grade, commercial decision support

**Parameters:**
- Area: 500–2,500 km²
- Resolution: 100m (medium)
- Live streaming: Included (WebSocket + replay)
- Digital twin: Included (depth probability + cross-section + isosurface)
- Analog validation: Included (top 3 analog deposits)
- Report depth: Standard (9 full sections: executive, spatial, system model, targets, digital twin, analog, resource, uncertainty, strategy)
- Data room access: 90 days

**Base Price:** $7,500  
**Area multiplier:** 1.0 + (area_km² / 1500) × 0.001  
**Resolution multiplier:** 1.0 (baseline at 100m)  
**Live streaming add-on:** Already included; no surcharge

**Price Calculation Example (1,500 km² AOI, 100m resolution):**
- Base: $7,500
- Area adjustment: 1,500/1500 × 0.001 = 0.001 = 1% increase → $75
- Resolution: 1.0 (baseline)
- **Total: $7,575**

**Feature additions:**
- Report depth upgrade to Executive: +$1,500
- Data room extension (90 more days): +$300

**Total Standard tier: $7,575–$9,375**

### Tier 3: EXECUTIVE
**Target:** Investment-grade opportunity, capital deployment, government/sovereign decision-making

**Parameters:**
- Area: 2,500–10,000 km²
- Resolution: 20–50m (fine multispectral)
- Live streaming: Included + custom replay speeds
- Digital twin: Advanced (full voxel grid + uncertainty surfaces)
- Analog validation: Extended (top 5 analogs + confidence-weighted synthesis)
- Report depth: Executive grade (same 9 sections + detailed audit trail + sensitivity analysis)
- Data room access: 180 days
- Custom visualizations: 3D fly-through, interactive maps
- Analyst support: 2 hours of post-report consultation

**Base Price:** $25,000  
**Area multiplier:** 1.0 + (area_km² / 5000) × 0.0015  
**Resolution multiplier (20m):** 250/20 = 12.5x (multiplier for finer resolution)  
**Resolution multiplier (50m):** 250/50 = 5.0x

**Price Calculation Example (5,000 km² AOI, 50m resolution):**
- Base: $25,000
- Area adjustment: 5,000/5000 × 0.0015 = 0.0015 = 0.15% → negligible
- Resolution multiplier: 5.0 → $25,000 × 5.0 = $125,000
- **Total: $150,000** (primarily driven by fine resolution)

**Feature additions:**
- Data room extension (180 more days): +$500
- Custom analyst briefing (additional 2 hours): +$750
- Source data package (raw satellite + processed): +$2,000

**Total Executive tier: $150,000–$153,250**

## 6. DISCOUNTS & BULK INCENTIVES

### Multi-Scan Discount
- **2–3 scans:** 5% discount on total
- **4–5 scans:** 10% discount on total
- **6+ scans:** 15% discount on total (per annum)

### Long-term Partnership
- **Annual retainer:** Includes up to 4 scans/year; 10% discount vs. a la carte
- Example: $50,000 retainer = ~4 standard-tier scans at ~25% savings

### Academic / Non-Profit
- **50% discount** on all tiers (proof of status required)

### Government / Sovereign
- **Negotiated rates** based on volume + policy considerations
- Typical: 20–30% discount for national exploration agencies

## 9. PAYMENT TERMS & BILLING

### Standard Terms
- **Invoice date:** Upon delivery of report
- **Payment due:** 30 days net
- **Currency:** USD (other currencies at prevailing FX rate)
- **Late payment penalty:** 1.5% per month

### Retainer Model
- **Annual prepayment:** 10% discount vs. quarterly billing
- **Unused credits:** Carried forward 12 months; forfeited if not used
- **Example retainer:** $50,000/year includes 4 standard-tier scans

### Government/Institutional
- **Payment on account:** NET 60–90 (by authorization)
- **Volume contracts:** Custom terms negotiated

## 10. WHAT'S NOT INCLUDED (EXPLICIT EXCLUSIONS)

- **Well drilling:** Aurora report is advisory only; operator bears all drilling costs
- **Permitting support:** Aurora does not manage environmental/government approvals
- **Operational exploration:** Ground surveys, outcrops, core analysis—operator responsibility
- **Mining/resource modeling:** Beyond report scope; requires third-party engineering
- **Legal/regulatory advice:** Aurora does not provide legal or compliance guidance
- **Title research:** Mineral rights verification is operator responsibility
